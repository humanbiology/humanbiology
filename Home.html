Our Health Mission

Human Biology is tasked with multi-tiered homeostasis among the African diaspora through interstate food desert transformation into biomimetic ecosystems. Our approach emphasizes precolonial whole food plant-based culinary medicine and patient engagement where success is non-communicable disease regression on a public health scale.
